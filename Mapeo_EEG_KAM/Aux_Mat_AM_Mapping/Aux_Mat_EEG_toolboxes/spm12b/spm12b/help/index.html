Welcome to SPM12b


This is SPM12b, the beta version of the next major release of SPM:

http://www.fil.ion.ucl.ac.uk/spm/software/spm12/

Public updates are taking place frequently until the final release. You can update your copy of SPM by using spm_update.m (check for update).

We are always interested to hear feedbacks and comments from SPM users - please contact us at <fil.spm@ucl.ac.uk>.

If you happen to find any bug, please report them at the same email address. Thank you!
